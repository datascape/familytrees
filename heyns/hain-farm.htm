Death record of Johan Peter Heins in 1770 (from the church records of Buecken):

 

„1770

24. Calle den 15 Jun. Johan Peter Heins, sonst Haars genant, starb

den 12. Jun. alt 69 Jahr 4 Monate und etliche Wochen.

Den Personalien nach ist er zu Wohlenhausen im Kirchspiel Lohe 1701. geboren

und hat sein Vater Peter Freuershaus, und seine Mutter Anna

Elisabeth Heins geheißen.“

 

Translation:

 

„1770

24. Calle on 15th of June Johan Peter Heins, also called Haars, died

on 12th of June, age 69 years 4 months and several weeks.

As to the particulars he was born in Wohlenhausen, (Mark-)Lohe parish in 1701 and

his father was called Peter Freuershaus and his mother was called Anna Elisabeth Heins.”
